Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Everything you need to know about our medical courier services

General Information

What types of deliveries do you handle?

We specialize in delivering medical supplies, pharmaceuticals, lab specimens, blood samples, and other sensitive items that require fast, secure, and temperature-controlled transport.

Are your couriers trained for medical deliveries?

Yes, all of our couriers undergo extensive training to handle medical deliveries in compliance with industry standards and regulations. They are well-versed in the safe transport of pharmaceuticals, biological specimens, and medical equipment.

Do you offer temperature-controlled delivery?

Yes, we provide temperature-controlled services for deliveries that require specific temperature conditions such as refrigerated or frozen goods, including medications and biological samples.

How do I track my shipment?

We provide real-time GPS tracking for all shipments. Once your package is in transit, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS, which you can use to monitor the delivery progress.

Safety & Security

How do you ensure the safety of my items?

We follow strict guidelines to ensure that all medical deliveries are secure, including temperature monitoring, tamper-evident packaging, and compliance with regulatory standards. All of our drivers are background-checked and trained to handle sensitive medical items securely.

What happens if my package is delayed?
